about us

«Rose and Margarita» — this is not just flowers, this is emotions, packed in petals.

We create bouquets that say more than any words. Each of our flowers — this is a story, each composition — an admission of feelings. We carefully select fresh flowers, so they can delight the eye and heart for as long as possible.

In our shop you will find both gentle field bouquets and elegant compositions for special occasions. We believe that flowers can bring joy and inspiration, so we pay attention to every detail: from color selection to packaging.
